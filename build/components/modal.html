Modal

Jazcss utilises Remodal for its modal component. Click this to see it in action.

I am a modal!

And this is how you can use it in the Handlebars views:

<a href="#mymodal">Click this</a>

{{#extend "modal" modal-id="mymodal"}}
    {{#content "modal-content"}}
        I am a modal!
    {{/content}}
{{/extend}}

Or use it directly:

<a href="#mymodal">Click this</a>

<div class="remodal" data-remodal-id="#mymodal">
    I am a modal!
    <button data-remodal-action="close" class="reset remodal-close"></button>
</div>
